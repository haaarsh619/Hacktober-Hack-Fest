Rules & Prizes

In this 24-hour long hackthon, participants have to make as many contributions as possible to the public projects/repos. on GitHub specified by the event organizers and judges. Only quality contributions will be counted, the team/person with maximum number of contribution will be the winner. In case of tie, the team with more quality contribution which will be decided on the site by judges will be the winner, decision solely lies in the hands of judges.

All the winners get official software licenses, goodies, swag etc. Prizes will be declared onsite.
